OVERVIEW

The Problem

Leisure travel is an indispensable routine for many people nowadays that helps them escape from a busy schedule and reward themselves after working hard. It is expected to refresh and revitalize the traveler’s’ body and soul. However, almost every individual experiences some level of travel stress during their planned trip, such as failing to deal with unexpected situations. Getting suitable recommendations presents to be another challenge. How to edit or adjust travel plans accordingly to alleviate people’s travel stress and to help travelers obtain better travel experience is worthy of consideration.


The Solution

We are designing a smartwatch and smartphone paired application, MOGO, that takes an individual’s travel itinerary as its input, learns their personal preferences along the way to provide helpful travel tips and re-plans individual’s trip whenever needed.
