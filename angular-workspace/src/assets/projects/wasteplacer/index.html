wasteplacer

A smart bin solution to assist you manage your waste. Encourages correct sorting, gives helpful tips, and allows you to see your habits change over time!


Tell Me More

Problem

People tend to show a lack of understanding of their impact when it comes to waste. A combination of incorrect sorting, overconsumption, and apathy all contribute to harmful habits.

Solution

Wasteplacer is a smart bin system along with a camera and screen to assist with correct sorting, as well as helping to improve habits and reduce environmental impact.

The System

Take a quick look at the wasteplacer smart bin system.

Smart Bins

A person walking up to wasteplacer will see your standard three bins along with the interactive panel and item scanner above it, which they can use to assist in sorting. Additionally, the front of the bins show previously incorrectly sorted items!

Interactive Panel

The interactive panel serves two main purposes. First, being able to see your waste habits over time through the track progress button. Secondly, assistance in sorting which utilizes the item scanner.

Item Scanner

The item scanner is able to determine which bin an item should be placed. After an item is disposed of, an internal scanner will double check the placement, allowing wasteplacer to ensure correct placement.



Feedback

Correct disposal of an item flashes a green screen, increments the streak, as well as giving a helpful tip on how to further improve your habits!

Incorrect disposal of an item flashes a red screen, zeroes the streak, as well as giving a fact to motivate correct disposal!

Design Process

See how we got to where we are!

  • Design Research

    Early on, we decided that we wanted to understand the impact that waste has on our world. We wanted to learn how people used and disposed of their waste, and try and understand why they did so.

  • Initial Design

    After conducting our research, we came up with three primary designs, including two different smart bin designs and a smart phone application. We originally tried moving forward with the phone application, with a main focus of sorting waste.

  • Pivot to Smart Bin

    After receiving lots of feedback from our users, we realized a phone application may have been too heavyweight. We pivoted to smart bin in order to make the process more efficient. We further refined our smart bin after several heuristic evaluations and usability tests.

  • Finalizing wasteplacer

    After finalizing our prototype for our final product, wasteplacer, we were able to convert our paper prototype into a full digital mockup!
