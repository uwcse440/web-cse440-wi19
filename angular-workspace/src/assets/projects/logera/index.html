Overview

A modern tool for travel logging

Photographs are today's primary medium for documenting the world as we see it. Especially with travelers, we often see photos used to capture particular moments so that they may endure, even when those memories start to fade.

When reminiscing on past travels, people often rely on photos that were taken during their trips to give context to the stories they share. However, over time, these stories lose details. A single snapshot of a moment cannot give enough context to tell a lasting story, so Logera helps to enrich this medium to make stories last.

Task 1

Using digital photos to document moments during a trip with rich contextual information.

With Logera, documenting your experiences in the moment is no longer limtied to photographing. With just a tap of a button, you can add a quick caption to your photo, describing where you are, who you're with, or what your favorite part of this moment is. When you look back at these photos a few years down the line, you won't have to guess what the context was, you'll be able to see exactly what you were thinking in the moment.

Task 2

Journaling thoughts and experiences after a day of traveling.

Journaling isn't for everyone. We understand that the end of a long day, it can be tough to find time to sit down and write out the highlights of your travels. Logera is here to help you get the best of journaling, without the extra effort. Start a journal whenever you have a few minutes to spare, and just respond to a few simple prompts about the places you visited and photos you took throughout the day.
