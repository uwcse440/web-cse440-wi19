Record Mood in Class

Connect the penguin attachment to any pen, pencil, or stylus to make recording your mood as easy as pressing a single button. Don't worry, the penguin attachment will remind you to do so if too much time has passed.

View Mood Records

Once you've recorded your mood in class, open up the penguin mobile app to see all your recordings. They're conveniently organized by class and lecture so you can see exactly where you need to focus.

Connect to Resources

The penguin app will easily connect you to your notes from class and to Panopto recordings of your lectures. If more help is needed, penguin can help you get in touch with course staff.

Features

The magic of penguin lies in how much it can do for you while being far less distracting than using your phone in class. Here are just some of the ways penguin can help you to begin succeeding in your classes each and every time.

Take it Anywhere

We won't tell you how you should take notes. You can attach penguin to any pen, pencil, or stylus.

Simple, Intuitive Buttons

Click any of the three buttons to make a recording. Feel free to jot down any notes you think you'll need later as well.

Synced with Courses

Simply login to the penguin app with your school's account so your recordings automatically sync with your classes.

View and Update Moods

Within the app, you can view your recordings. You can edit them if there are mistakes or your feelings change.

Watch Panopto Recordings

If you find that you are struggling with a topic, penguin will easily connect you to the lecture recordings for that topic.

Email Course Staff

When you need personalized help, penguin makes it really easy to reach out to course staff with questions.
